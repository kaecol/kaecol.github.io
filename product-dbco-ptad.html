DBCO PTAD Functionalization Kit

Product Overview

The DBCO PTAD Kit is an essential tool for functionalizing dibenzocyclooctyne (DBCO) groups to proteins, enabling various click chemistry applications like conjugation and labeling. It uses activated 4-phenyl-1,2,4-triazoline-3,5-dione (PTAD) linkers to react with tyrosine or tryptophan residues. PTAD selectively reacts with tyrosine or tryptophan, depending on the pH.

Ideal for drug development, diagnostics, and biochemical research, this kit offers a simple and effective way to enhance protein functionality.

Purpose

DBCO functionalization

Mechanism

Reacting with tyrosine or tryptophan depending on pH

Applications

Protein conjugation & labelling
