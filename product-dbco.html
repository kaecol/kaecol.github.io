Click Chemistry & SPAAC

If you need fast, reliable solutions for connecting molecules in your research or product development, click chemistry offers exactly that. It's widely used in drug discovery and bioconjugation because of its efficiency and ease of use. Whether you're looking to create stable, functional biomolecules or simplify your processes, click chemistry provides a straightforward way to get the results you need.

Strain-promoted azide-alkyne cycloaddition (SPAAC): For customers working in sensitive environments like with certain therapeutic proteins or living cells, SPAAC allows you to label and modify biomolecules without disrupting biological functions. It’s a great option for applications like molecular imaging or targeted drug delivery.

DBCO Functionalization

If you're looking to easily prepare biomolecules for labelling or conjugation, our DBCO functionalization solutions are designed to help. By adding DBCO groups to your biomolecules, you can ensure they’re ready for use in click chemistry reactions with azide-molecules, allowing you to streamline complex tasks like protein labelling or drug delivery.
