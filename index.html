About Us

We are a research group in the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, led by Prof Tan Lay Poh. The goal of our laboratory is to develop and apply engineering tools to study and control the interface between stem cells and biomaterials.

Prof. Tan Lay Poh

Principal Investigator

Ph.D in Materials Science and Engineering
Associate Professor Tan Lay Poh is currently in the School of Materials Science and Engineering since 2004. Her research interests include cultivated meat, polymer engineering, biodegradable polymers and heart tissue engineering.

Profile


Dr. Tan Pei Leng

Research Fellow

Ph.D in Materials Science and Engineering
Dr. Tan Pei Leng main project on cultivated meat is focused on the engineering of fat cells and scaling up of the platform for commercialisation purposes. Her side project involves looking into the sustained release of nutrients for the stimulation of plant growth. Her research interests currently include sustainability research in cultivated meat, agriculture and waste valorization, as well as translational biomedical research in tissue engineering and medical device fabrication. Pei Leng enjoys swimming, water polo, managing her social media account and creating personal content videos.
peileng.tan@ntu.edu.sg
